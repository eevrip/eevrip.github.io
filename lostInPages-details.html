Lost In Pages

This was the second game jam I participated in. This time I wanted to challenge myself to create everything from the design and programming to the art. The music and sound effects were created by a musician I met in a previous jam.

Theme

The theme was "Calm before the storm". I wanted to create a puzzle game for this jam, before even I knew the theme. So I tried to figure out a way to encapsulate the theme to this genre. So I decided to create a story based puzzle game: the player will unravel the story while solving the puzzles. I also wanted to make something unique, so I decided that the storm will be an emotional one.

Story

Read your journal to unravel your memories. Each page you read is messy and confusing. You are trying to piece together the information and remember. As you unravel more information you realise that you suffer from an irriversible condition that relates to memory loss and confusion.
