Dude, where is my car?

In this jam I participated with a team. I got in touch with a programmer from the thatgamecompany x COREBLAZER jam and we decided to team up. Jeroen is using Godot and so we decided to work in this engine which was new to me. I also found a very talented artist, Inane! Jeroen also introduced me to Saddie who is a musician and Elliot an aspiring artist. Together we developed this 2D/3D hybrid game. Though the game didn't include sfx and other small details that would make it polished at the time, I had fun developing it and I love the end product we created within 72 hours.

Theme

The theme was "Loop". We came up with the idea of hiking. When you go for a hike you have to come back to your car in the end. So that is a form of a loop.

Story

Froggy has a list of a number of spots that he wants to visit but he wants to return to his car in the end. Help him find a way to do that!

Post Jam

Jeroen and I are still working on this game to polish it, to add what we had missed before the deadline and refine some features.
